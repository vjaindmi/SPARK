3.   One-Key App Functionality
The One-Key App provides functions for tool control (see clause 3.2), inventory management (see clause 3.3) and tool reporting (see clause 3.4) in connection with certain Milwaukee tools and equipment that is One-Key ready (“One-Key Tool”).
The One-Key App allows to set certain parameters in One-Key Tools, e.g. the speed or torque of a drill. Commands are sent by the Device to the One-Key Tool via a wireless Bluetooth connection. Please refer to the manual of Your tool to find out if and to what extent it can be used in connection with the One-Key App. The tool control functionality of the One-Key App can be used as a stand-alone function, i.e. without using the inventory management system or tool reporting.
The One-Key App contains inventory management functions (i) to create an inventory of One-Key Tools and third party tools, and (ii) to gather and manage information about the usage of such tools and to create respective reports. These functions are provided by the One-Key App, however certain functions may only be available in connection with an online inventory management platform (website) (“Inventory Management Platform”). If You use the inventory management functions, the One-Key App will automatically collect from registered One-Key Tools certain data (e.g. serial number, use history) via Bluetooth, store such data and send it via an internet connection of the Device (if available) to the server of the Inventory Management Platform (“One-Key Server”).
This Agreement does not cover the use of the Inventory Management Platform (website). For the terms and conditions of the Inventory Management Platform please see [Link#02]. We reserve the right to cancel the connectivity of the One-Key App to the One-Key Server at any time and do not owe a specific availability of the One-Key Server.
The One-Key App can gather information about the One-Key Tool and its usage (“Tool Data”), provide respective reports (as the case may be in connection with the Inventory Management Platform) and notification about recommended maintenance.
In order to use the One-Key App You have to create an online account (“One-Key Account”). Such registration requires an internet connection and can be accomplished within the One-Key App. In order to use the One-Key App in connection with a specific One-Key Tool, the One-Key Tool (ID) must be linked with Your One-Key Account.
In order to use the One-Key App Your Device must fulfill certain hard- and software requirements (e.g. version of operating system, etc.). Please see for details [Link#03].
We may provide from time to time updates of the One-Key App which may also include bug-fixes. It may also be necessary to install an update in order to ensure interoperability of the One-Key App with future versions of the Inventory Management Platform or future One-Key Tools. We therefore strongly recommend installing updates. This Agreement does not create an obligation of Us to provide any updates or updates with a specific functionality. Your statutory rights remain unaffected.
